Blender Recipes

  1. Carrot Ginger Soup

    This warm and comforting soup is made with carrots, ginger, and spices. Simply blend the ingredients together in your blender and heat until warm.

  2. Butternut Squash Soup

    This creamy and delicious soup is made with roasted butternut squash, onions, and spices. Blend the ingredients together in your blender until smooth and heat until warm.

  3. Green Goddess Smoothie

    Start your day with a boost of vitamins and nutrients by blending together spinach, kale, avocado, and your favorite fruits and nuts.

  4. Spicy Tomato Gazpacho

    This cold soup is made with ripe tomatoes, bell peppers, cucumbers, and spices. Blend the ingredients together in your blender and chill until ready to serve.

  5. Eggplant Caponata

    This traditional Sicilian dish is made with eggplant, onions, olives, and capers. Blend the ingredients together in your blender until smooth and serve as a dip or topping for bread or crackers.

  6. Roasted Red Pepper Hummus

    This creamy and flavorful dip is made with roasted red peppers, chickpeas, garlic, and lemon juice. Blend the ingredients together in your blender until smooth and serve with veggies or pita chips.

Salted Fish Recipes

  1. Herring Salad

    This classic Russian dish is made with salted herring, potatoes, carrots, and onions. Soak the herring in cold water for several hours to remove excess salt, then mix with the chopped vegetables and dress with mayonnaise and vinegar.

  2. Capelin Caviar

    This delicious spread is made with salted capelin roe, lemon juice, and spices. Soak the capelin in cold water for several hours to remove excess salt, then blend with the other ingredients in a food processor until smooth.

  3. Sprat Toasts

    These tasty and easy appetizers are made with salted sprats, bread, and garlic. Soak the sprats in cold water for several hours to remove excess salt, then spread onto slices of bread and bake until golden brown.

Bread Maker Recipes

  1. Whole Wheat Bread

    This healthy and delicious bread is made with whole wheat flour, yeast, salt, sugar, and water. Simply add the ingredients to your bread maker and select the whole wheat setting for a fresh and fragrant loaf.

  2. French Bread

    This classic bread is made with bread flour, yeast, salt, sugar, and water. The bread maker will take care of kneading and rising the dough, then you can transfer it to a baking sheet to bake in the oven for a crispy crust.

  3. Sourdough Bread

    This tangy and rustic bread is made with sourdough starter, bread flour, yeast, salt, and water. Combine the ingredients in your bread maker, then transfer the dough to a greased bowl to rise for several hours before baking in a preheated oven.
